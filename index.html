About

Built on range

Versatility isn't a side note in Gavin's training — it's the whole point.

Gavin has been dancing since he was five, when he started with a group hip-hop routine at Body Expressions. He added a new style nearly every year, and after three years earned a place on the company competitive team.

From there he kept widening his reach — joining a men's advanced ballet studio for classical line and control, while continuing to climb through the competitive circuit across every style. Advanced tumbling sharpened his tricks, and one-on-one hip-hop and breaking sessions shaped the signature style he carries today.

He's collected top awards and scholarships along the way. Now, heading into his senior year, his sights are set on the next stage: a high-level collegiate dance program and a professional career — all while aiming to finish high school above a 4.0 GPA.
